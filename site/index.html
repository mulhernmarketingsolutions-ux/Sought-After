Still Deciding?

Questions we get on almost every call.

Do I own everything when we're done?

Completely. Your logo, brand files, website, all of it — nothing is licensed back to us, and nothing disappears if we ever stop working together. It's yours, full stop.

Can I update the website myself later?

Depends on what you need. Simple changes — photos, prices, seasonal updates — we can set you up to handle on your own. Prefer not to deal with it? We offer ongoing care plans so it's always just a message away. Either way, you're never stuck waiting on us to move.

Is 1 week a real number, or a marketing one?

Real — it's the same day-by-day breakdown above: strategy, identity, build, launch. Want the full growth system on top of it — lead gen, ongoing management, hands-on support? That runs 4–6 weeks. Either way, no half-year branding sagas.

I already have a logo or some brand assets — do I start from scratch?

Not at all. If parts of your brand are already working, we build around them. This is about closing the gap between where you are and where you should be — not throwing out what's already good.

Not sure which package fits me?

That's exactly what the free call is for. We'll tell you honestly which tier makes sense — even when that means recommending the smaller one.

Are these custom designs, or templates?

Custom, every time. Nothing here comes from a theme with your logo dropped in — it's built around your brand specifically.

What happens after launch — are you still around?

Yes. The Full System includes hands-on support and site management for the length of the engagement, and ongoing care plans are available afterward if you'd like us on call long-term.
